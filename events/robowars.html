Robo Wars

For Classes - OPEN

Teams Per School - 1

Participants per Team - 2



Robot Specifications

1- The bot should fit in a box of dimensions 1.5ft X 1.5ft X 1.5ft (l X b X h).

2-The maximum allowed weight of the robot must not exceed 10kgs.

3-A maximum of 24 volts DC is allowed for powering the robot.

4-The robot could be controlled with wired or wireless remote. Wire should be at least 5m long.

5-Robots can have any kind of magnetic weapon, cutters, flippers, saws, lifting devices.

Event Rules

1 The competition will be played on a knock-out basis.

2 The maximum duration of each round will be 5 minutes.

3 Any team that is not ready at the time specified will be given a maximum time of 5 minutes to get ready.

4 The machine would be checked for its safety before the competition and would be discarded if found unsafe for other participants and spectators.

5 The organizers reserve the rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any will be highultrabolded on the website and notified to the registered teams

6 Judge’s decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
